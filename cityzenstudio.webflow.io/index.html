How does Cityzen handle data backups? Are they automated?

Cityzen employs an automated backup system, ensuring data is periodically backed up without manual intervention. These backups are stored in secure, encrypted formats across multiple locations, ensuring data integrity and availability even in unforeseen circumstances.

How customizable are the visual elements of Cityzen's platform?

Cityzen provides extensive customization options for visual elements. From themes, fonts, and color palettes to more intricate design elements, cities can tailor the platform's appearance to resonate with their brand identity and local culture.

How does Cityzen ensure its platform remains compliant with accessibility standards?

Cityzen is built in line with global web accessibility standards. Our platform undergoes regular accessibility audits to ensure compliance with standards such as WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines). This ensures that the platform remains accessible to all citizens, including those with disabilities.

How long does it typically take to get our city website live using Cityzen?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project, custom features required, and the volume of data migration. However, our streamlined onboarding process and experienced team ensure timely delivery. Typically, most city websites can go live within a few weeks of initiating the project.

What makes Cityzen stand out from other city website platforms?

Cityzen, with its deep-rooted experience, commitment to innovation, and holistic approach, offers cities more than just a digital platform. It's a partnership, a promise of digital excellence. Our meticulous onboarding, emphasis on security, and continuous support make Cityzen the premier choice for cities aiming for digital distinction.

How customizable is the Cityzen platform for individual city needs?

Cityzen is built on a framework that is both robust and flexible. While it comes packed with features essential for every city, it's also designed to accommodate specific customizations. Whether it's unique modules, integrations with existing systems, or special design elements, Cityzen can be tailored to meet the unique heartbeat of your city.

How does the onboarding process work with Cityzen?

Cityzen offers a meticulous onboarding process that includes stakeholder interviews, asset aggregation, deep research, and citizen-centric polls. This holistic approach ensures that the resulting platform aligns perfectly with both administrative goals and citizen needs. From strategy to implementation, our team of experts guides cities every step of the way, ensuring a seamless transition and a platform that resonates.
